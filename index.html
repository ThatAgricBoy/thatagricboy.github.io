Our Story

The inspiration behind Rently comes from the frustrating experiences many of us face when searching for a new place to call home. I found myself paying hefty inspection fees to view properties that didn't meet my preferences and dealing with numerous agents, each adding their fees to the cost. Finally, when I discovered a property I loved, the agent fees nearly equaled the rent itself. It was clear that there had to be a better way.

Our team dedicated three weeks to bring this vision to life, working collaboratively to create a platform that makes finding your ideal rental property easier and more affordable.
